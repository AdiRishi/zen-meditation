# Moss Product

## Product Description

Moss is a quiet meditation companion designed to make regular practice easier. It replaces abrasive phone timers with a calm, minimally interruptive session and supports commitment through flexible goals, subtle reminders, and encouraging progress.

## Core Experience

### Meditate

- Choose a duration and begin with minimal setup.
- Keep the session fully silent and free from required interaction.
- End with a soft, subtle sound rather than an alarm.
- Return the person to the rest of their day gently.

### Build a rhythm

- Set an intended practice cadence, such as sessions per day or days per week.
- Receive restrained, configurable reminders before planned sessions.
- Make it easy to begin when a reminder arrives.

### See progress

- Show completed sessions and consistency over time.
- Use streaks, simple charts, or similar summaries to make progress tangible.
- Celebrate commitment without punishing missed sessions.

## Product Character

Moss should feel quiet, elegant, and focused. Each screen should have a clear purpose, strong visual hierarchy, and little competition around the primary action. Motion and sound should be subtle. Language should be warm, concise, and free from urgency.

The app should recede during meditation and provide gentle structure outside it.

## Initial Boundaries

The initial product is not:

- a guided-meditation app;
- a meditation content library;
- an ambient music or soundscape player during sessions.

## Decisions Still to Make

- How practice goals are expressed: sessions per day, days per week, or both.
- How reminders relate to a schedule and how much flexibility they allow.
- Which session durations are offered and whether custom durations are supported.
- Which progress views best encourage regularity without creating pressure.
- Whether people choose from several gentle completion sounds.
